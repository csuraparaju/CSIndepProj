Welcome to part one of this process. To begin with, please record an audio file of you speaking about a topic or an experience that you have recently encountered. This is quote vague, and it can be about anything you like. Some examples include discussing a book or a T.V. show.



Once you are done recording, please upload the speech recording below. Ensure that the files are in the .wav audio format. If your file is not in the correct format, there are online resources that can help you convert from ine format to others. Simply search "convery audio file to .wav" online and you will get multiple results.



Part one




Once you press submit, a loading animation will appear in the browser's top bar. This means that the program is analyzing the .wav file and is determining a score for this part. Please be patient as this process may take up to a full minute. I apologize for the long load times.
